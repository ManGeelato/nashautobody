What We Do

Panel Beating Services

All our autobody repair and panel beating work is performed by skilled technicians with onsite supervision and quality management. Our services include complete vehicle body restoration, dent removal, spray painting, scratch repair, and frame alignment to restore your vehicle to its original condition.

If your vehicle needs repairs, we’re here to assist. With our autobody and panel beating services, we use state-of-the-art equipment and provide high-quality materials such as premium automotive paints, primers, and replacement parts.

All our autobody repair and panel beating work is carried out by trained professionals to ensure precision and customer satisfaction.

Vehicle Restoration & Repairs

We specialize in intricate and hard-to-reach repairs, such as restoring damage to vehicle frames, hard-to-access body panels, and undercarriage components. All our technicians are fully qualified and adhere to the highest safety and quality standards, ensuring repairs are conducted effectively and efficiently.

In this category, we handle frame straightening, panel replacement, rust removal, specialized welding, and precision dent repairs.

Our restoration and repair services are performed with the help of advanced equipment, including hydraulic lifts, precision frame straighteners, spray booths, and high-tech diagnostic tools to ensure accurate and safe repairs for your vehicle. We also offer specialized repair techniques for unique projects, such as restoring vintage or luxury vehicles, ensuring every detail is handled with precision. Give us a call for all your vehicle repair and restoration needs.
